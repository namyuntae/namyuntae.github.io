What makes a hero?

Heroes are people we respect. They also show us what values we value the most in our lives. That's why we can get a glimpse of important values in a person's life if we look at individual heroes.

Michael Jackson revolutionized the world of pop music. He led the way to end an era of mere listening and satisfying music with his ears and to open an era of "viewing music" that satisfies both eyes and ears at the same time. He made a global impact with this modernized dance pop and completely destroyed every wall regardless of race, age, or gender. He has made a huge impact on the world of pop music and given emotional comfort and happiness to many people.
